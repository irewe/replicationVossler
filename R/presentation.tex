\subsection{Report no. of alternatives, attributes, levels,
block}\label{report-no.-of-alternatives-attributes-levels-block}

\begin{itemize}
\item
  2 alternatives for each choice set
\item
  4 attributes (spatial scale, BCG change scenario, Location, annual tax
  increase)
\item
  Levels per attribute: (3, 4, 2, 10)
\item
  Five blocks
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Total number of possible choice
set}\label{total-number-of-possible-choice-set}

(3\emph{4}2*10)\^{}2 = 57 600

\subsection{Blocks}\label{blocks}

They had 10 blocks (in two different groups). In one group of blocks
they kept spatial unit (combination of spatial scale and location)
constant, in the other group they kept the change in environmental
status constant. Each respondent got assigned to 2 blocks, either in
spatial or the environmental category.

\subsubsection{Spatial unit constant (5
blocks):}\label{spatial-unit-constant-5-blocks}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Single watershed + Policy area includes home watershed (local)
\item
  Single watershed + Policy area does not includes home watershed
  (non-local)
\item
  Three contiguous watersheds + Policy area includes home watershed
  (local)
\item
  Three contiguous watersheds + Policy area does not includes home
  watershed (non-local)
\item
  Full study region (including home)
\end{itemize}
